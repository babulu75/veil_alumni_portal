Declaration of Commitment

Veil AI reaffirms its commitment to transparency, inclusion, and responsible innovation. This declaration reflects our values as an organization that prioritizes ethical technology, user privacy, and alumni engagement.

Ethical Use of Technology

We pledge to use artificial intelligence and related technologies in a responsible manner that promotes user safety, transparency, and fairness. Our AI tools are designed to respect user dignity and cultural norms.

Respect for Privacy and Security

Our platform follows robust privacy and security frameworks. User data is not used for profiling or targeted advertising. We practice data minimization, end-to-end encryption, and accountability.

Inclusive Community Engagement

We believe alumni engagement must be inclusive and diverse. We welcome feedback from all users to help build a vibrant and respectful digital community. Discrimination or harassment of any kind is strictly prohibited.

Accountability

We are committed to regular internal reviews and external audits of our practices. Any misconduct or policy violation will be addressed transparently and with due diligence.
