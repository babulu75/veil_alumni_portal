🔵 Header Section
Background: #002147 (Navy Blue)
→ Symbolizes trust, intelligence, and professionalism — perfect for universities
 and alumni platforms.

Logo Text: #FFFFFF (White)
→ High contrast for maximum readability.

Navigation Menu Links:

Default: #FFFFFF (White)

Hover/Active: #FFD700 (Gold)
→ Gold accent gives a premium and elegant feel.

Icons (e.g., hamburger menu, social media):

Color: #FFFFFF or #FFD700 on hover

⚪ Main Body Background
Background: #F5F5F5 (Light Gray/Off-White)
→ Soft on the eyes, professional, and neutral to help content stand out.

🔤 Typography (Body Text & Headings)
Body Text (Paragraphs, Descriptions):
Color: #333333 (Dark Charcoal)
→ Excellent readability, not as harsh as black.

Headings (H1, H2, H3, etc.):
Color: #001B3A (Dark Navy Blue)
→ Distinct from body text, provides hierarchy and attention.

Links (Inline):
Default: #002147 (Navy Blue)

Hover: #FFD700 (Gold) with underline
→ Clearly stands out as interactive without clashing.

🟨 Buttons
Primary Buttons (e.g., “Register”, “Join”, “Get Involved”):
Background: #FFD700 (Gold)

Text: #002147 (Navy Blue)

Hover Background: #FFC107 (Slightly darker Gold)

Border Radius: Rounded (8px–12px)

Secondary Buttons (e.g., “Learn More”):
Background: Transparent or #FFF8E1 (Light Gold Tint)

Text: #002147

Border: 1px solid #FFD700

Hover: Background becomes #FFD700, text turns white

📦 Content Sections (Cards, Quotes, Highlights)
Card Backgrounds:
Color: #FFFFFF (White)

Border: 1px solid #E0E0E0

Text: #333333

Title: #001B3A

Call-Out Box / Quote Box:
Background: #FFF8E1 (Very Light Gold Tint)

Text: #002147

Icon or Quote Symbol: #FFD700

📁 Projects or Events Section
Card Background: #FFFFFF

Title: #001B3A

Date/Info: #555555

Action Button: Use primary button style

Hover Card Border: 2px solid #FFD700 or subtle shadow

📮 Contact / Form Section
Form Background: #FFFFFF inside a section of #F5F5F5

Input Fields:

Border: 1px solid #CCCCCC

Text Color: #333333

Placeholder: #888888

Focus Border: #FFD700

Submit Button: Use Primary Button style

⚫ Footer Section
Background: #333333 (Dark Charcoal)

Text: #F5F5F5 (Off-White)

Links:

Default: #F5F5F5

Hover: #FFD700

Icons (Social Media, etc.):

Default: #F5F5F5

Hover: #FFD700

Copyright

Color: #BBBBBB (Muted Gray)

✅ Accessibility & Contrast Tips
Ensure contrast ratios meet WCAG AA standards.

Use tools like Color Contrast Checker for validation.
